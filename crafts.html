Crafts & Art

A collection of my craft projects (est. February 2025)

After signing up for a sewing training at the MIT Metropolis Makerspace, I discovered how much I loved crafting.
Within the span of a semester, I learned to sew, crochet, glass flamework, create stained glass, embroider, and so much more.
Now, inducted as a makerspace mentor, I continue to spend hours crafting every week and passing on my knowledge to others, continuing the sprit of making at MIT.
